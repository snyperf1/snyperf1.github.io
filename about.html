About

I design to make complex things feel simple.

My journey into Experience, Product and Design started with curiosity and became a long-term commitment to help people through better systems, visuals, and services.

How I Found XPD

Conversations That Changed Direction

At the MAD Grad Show and First Steps with SP programme, I spoke with lecturers and students about course expectations, studio culture, and project outcomes. Those conversations showed me how deeply XPD values empathy and iteration.

Why It Fits

I enjoy collaborative problem-solving, but I can also drive independent execution. That balance matches the way XPD combines research, prototyping, storytelling, and refinement.

"I love to help people, and design gives me a practical way to do exactly that."
