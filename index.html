Design Mindset

  • Listen first: identify user pain points before committing to visual solutions.
  • Prototype early: test concepts quickly with storyboards, decks, and low-fidelity models.
  • Iterate with feedback: improve clarity and usefulness through collaborative critique.
  • Deliver with care: ensure every detail supports usability and emotional resonance.
